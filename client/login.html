Sign in using Open ID? Why?

OpenID is the fast, easy and secure way to sign in to websites. Here are just a few benefits to using OpenID.

  • Accelerate Sign Up Process at Your Favorite Websites
  • Reduce Frustration Associated with Maintaining Multiple Usernames and Passwords
  • Gain Greater Control Over Your Online Identity
  • Minimize Password Security Risks
  • More at openid site

What is Open ID anyway?

Read more about OpenID authentication on the Open ID Foundation website.

OpenID is service that allows you to log-on to many different websites using a single indentity. Find out more about OpenID and how to get an OpenID enabled account.

What happens after I log in?

You will be taken to the AppLaud Register page. The second and last step before you can start creating Android apps.
Future visits to login will skip the register process.
